Grab the DMG.

Drag, double-click, done. Planto installs to /Applications and lives quietly in your menu bar from then on.

⬇ Download Planto.dmg
  1. Open Planto.dmg.
  2. Right-click Install Planto.command → Open.
  3. Approve the one-time Gatekeeper prompt.
  4. It installs to /Applications and launches — done forever.
